welcome to splitwise

Splitwise is a free and user-friendly app designed to help people easily split expenses with friends, family, or roommates. It simplifies the process of tracking shared expenses and settling up debts, making it easier to manage group finances and avoid conflicts. With Splitwise, users can create groups, add expenses, and split bills among the members, allowing everyone to keep track of who owes what to whom. The app also offers various features such as reminders, comments, and multiple currency support, making it an essential tool for anyone who frequently splits bills with others.
